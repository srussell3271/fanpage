Meet the Cast

Such amazing people who have different personalities and still are friends

Joseph Gatto,-Joe-,is my favorite cast member. I say that he is my favorite because we have the similar personality.I learned my boldness, risky, and funny traits because of him. He taught me to have fun with anything you do because it's like the meaning of life. Joe also taught me to just go for it, nothing can go wrong if you put your mind to it. Give or take life is to live for enjoy what you have now because the future holds your valuables. Friendship is Joe specialty, to him, his friends mean everything to him including his family.

Joseph Gatto was born in Staten Island, NY on June 5. He went to Monsignor Farrell High School and LIU Post. He has two older sisters. Joe Gatto is married to Bessy Gatto and they have a daughter, whose name is Milana.

Salvatore Vulcano -Sal- is my second favorite cast member. I say this because he has style that makes everything seems better. He can by anything and make it seem as if it's the newest trend or an outfit from a GQ Model. He also have this sensitive side to him that makes everyone more emotional. Sal has a sense of humor that can bring joy to anyone who is upset.

Salvatore Vulcano was born in Staten Island, NY on November 5. He went to Monsignor Farrell High School and St John's University Queens Campus. He has a younger sister Jenna, whom he is highly protective of.

James Murray -Murr- reminds me a planning tracker. He always tries to create a plan for this task and try to go from there. He always have a joyful attitude and loves to eat chicken noddle soup. He taught me to create a plan and even if you fail you can still try again. Eventually, you will succeed.

James Murray was born in Staten Island, NY on May 1. He went to Monsignor Farrell High School and Georgetown University. He is only child.

Brian Quinn -Q- reminds me of my character at home. We both enjoy being laid back and relaxed watching tv and eating. Quinn was a firefighter before Impractical Jokers and still is my hero now telling me anything can happen to change what you was doing before till now.

Brian Quinn was born in Staten Island, NY on March 14. He went to Monsignor Farrell High School and Brooklyn College. He is the only child.
